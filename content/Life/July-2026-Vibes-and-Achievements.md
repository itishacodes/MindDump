### 🏆 Big Wins & Achievements

* **Rebuilt the Digital Garden**: Cleaned out the entire git history of this blog to remove all 1,800+ upstream commits, making the repository completely mine and setting it up as a high-speed, lightweight personal brain dump.
* **Mastered Classical AI**: Built an A* pathfinding system, a single-neuron Perceptron, and a second-order Markov Chain text generator from scratch in pure JS.
* **Tamed Autonomous Agents**: Wrote a custom 40-line local ReAct agent loop and integrated a Loop Guard class to prevent token-devouring infinite compiler loops.
* **Secured Local Prompts**: Designed a separate Instruction Isolation Gate classifier to block malicious comment payloads from hijacking local shell scripts.
